About M-PAD Studios


Thank you for stopping by, my name is Mike and I'm the creator of M-PAD Studios.

After I graduated Western Oregon University with my Bachelor's Degree in Exercise Science I went on to work for several outpatient physical therapy clinics growing my experience and knowledge in the medical field.

M-PAD Studios was created to provide insight, quick tips, supplement knowledge, recipe guides, workout guides, physical therapy tips, mental health awareness, but with an organic feel to it.

Each blog topic I research and give a story of how it related to my life in hopes my readers can find a connection within it to theirs and benefit in a way to better their life.
