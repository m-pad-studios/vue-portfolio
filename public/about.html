About M-PAD Studios


Hi, and welcome to M-PAD Studios. My name is Mike and I'm the creator of M-PAD Studios Health & Fitness Blog.

My background is in Physical Therapy and Exercise Science with a Bachelor's Degree from Western Oregon University; however, I have experience from my time at a health food store helping customers in produce and supplements so I have an extensive background in working closely with people and help navigating their health.  M-PAD Studios was created with a vision to be able to help people all over the world with their health but in a simple way.

I wanted to be able to provide even more help and knowledge to fellow health enthuisiasts and I just felt like the blog wasn't enough. So, I started creating monthly PDF guides that are built using Canva and designed for those that want straight to the point help with weight loss, strength training, supplements, popular diet recipes (keto and paleo), mental health, and much more.

Collectively I have over +10 years experience in the health & fitness world and use that real world experience to make sure that a high quality product is delivered in each guide. Hopefully you find a guide that helps you out or just wanna send an email saying hi, either way thank you for stopping by and take care.

Cheers,

Mike.
