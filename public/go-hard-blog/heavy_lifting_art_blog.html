"Art of Lifting Heavy"


by Michael Parker

Look at that picture, it is just oozing with power, amazing. Doesn't that make you wanna go around and just start picking random things up, see if you're actually strong enough to? Straight up I know I'm guilty of this. I'm not sure what comes over me, could be a testoterone driven event occuring in my body at that very moment, no clue, but what I do know is that my pride is on the line if I don't pick whatever that heavy ass thing is in front of me up. That to me is what lifting heavy is all about, proving to one's self, to never back down from the challenge in front of you.

We have come a long way in learning how to move heavy objects, apparently as early as 3600 BC, safely and in a graceful manner almost as if a form of art. Of course art can be interpreted in many different ways but ask any weight lifter and they'll say that picture is art in its finest form.
