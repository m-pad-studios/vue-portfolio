"Stretch to Unwind"

by Michael Parker

Stretching, a great topic to debate in the workout world. I had teachers in college arguing almost entire class periods on how you should stretch or that you don't need to. One teacher flat out described stretching as worthless and a waste of time, that same teacher was our athletic trainer too. Then I graduate and go to work for several outpatient physical therapy clinics and almost every PT had their patients stretching.

Here's my experience and take on stretching: IT IS IMPORTANT!

Not quite sure what my teacher was yammering about that one day but they were wrong. For example, if you want to rehab a total knee replacement to get full range of motion back stretching is one of the main treatments.


Today let's look at a classic routine, used by body builders and strength trainers during needs of rapid muscle growth:

5 x 5 Workout A
Squats Sets: 5 Reps: 5
Bench Sets: 5 Reps: 5
Deadlift Sets: 1 or 5 (if you're more advanced) Reps: 5
5 x 5 Workout B
Squats Sets: 5 Reps: 5
Barbell Rows Sets: 5 Reps: 5
Landmine Press Sets: 5 Reps: 5
